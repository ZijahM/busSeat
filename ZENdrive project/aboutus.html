About us

ZENdrive is an online ticket purchasing website for bus transportation and its operations are mainly focused on providing the users with information about all available routes and allowing them to book them by using their accounts.


Our mission

To ensure the passengers' reliable booking by using our own human and technical potential on the principles of sustainable development and continuous monitoring of European quality.


To increase the quality of services towards our consumers and to ensure the satisfaction of our users.


To ensure continuous education of our employees.


To create an image of a socially responsible business entity.
